When Lucidchart is the better choice

SketchMyInfra is designed for speed and developer workflows — not for polished enterprise deliverables. Lucidchart is a better fit when:

  • You need real-time collaborative editing with multiple people in the same diagram
  • You need pixel-perfect layout control for a presentation or customer-facing deliverable
  • You need Confluence, Jira, or Salesforce integrations
  • You're creating non-infrastructure diagrams (org charts, flowcharts, ER diagrams)

For everything else — especially quick architecture documentation — SketchMyInfra is faster and free.
